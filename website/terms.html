Service Description

mpps.io provides a cryptographic attestation service for machine transactions. The service accepts SHA-256 content hashes, signs them with a hardware security module (HSM), and stores the resulting attestation records in immutable storage.

What We Are

An attestation service that provides cryptographic proof that a specific content hash was submitted at a specific point in time. Each attestation is HSM-signed (FIPS 140-2 Level 3) and stored in AWS S3 Object Lock (Compliance Mode) for 10 years.

What We Are NOT

  • Not a legal notary service — attestations are not legally binding notarizations
  • Not a content validator — we do not evaluate, verify, or endorse the content behind any hash
  • Not a financial guarantee — attestations do not guarantee the value, delivery, or quality of any transaction
  • Not a dispute resolution service — we provide evidence, not adjudication
